# AI HR Automation Examples

A practical collection of AI-assisted People Operations workflows, prompts, and automation templates designed to reduce manual HR work, improve consistency, and support scalable remote teams.

## Purpose

This repository demonstrates how AI can support HR Operations, People Analytics, Performance Management, onboarding, OKR reviews, and organizational execution.

The goal is not to replace human judgment, but to reduce repetitive work, improve consistency, and help People Operations teams make better decisions faster.

## Focus Areas

- Performance review summaries
- Manager insight prompts
- HR analytics prompts
- OKR review workflows
- Evaluation reminder logic
- Onboarding automation
- Slack notification concepts
- Jira and Confluence workflow ideas
- Structured decision-support templates

## Repository Structure

/prompts  
AI prompt templates for HR and People Operations workflows.

/workflows  
Operational workflow examples for reminders, evaluations, onboarding, and review cycles.

## Positioning

People Operations is becoming more system-driven, data-informed, and AI-assisted.

This repository shows practical ways to connect HR workflows, AI prompts, and operational systems.
